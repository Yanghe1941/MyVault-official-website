User Guide

MyVault is a local-first iOS app for managing real-world assets and keepsakes. This guide focuses on the common workflows.

1. Getting Started

Open the app and complete the onboarding pages. In Settings, select your Theme (Black Gold recommended), Language, and Currency. For extra privacy, enable the Face ID / Touch ID app lock.

2. Adding Items

Tap the "+" icon to record item details. Beyond brand and price, we recommend adding receipts or photos. Use the "Story" field to preserve the memories or the purpose behind your purchase.

3. Organization

Categories: Build a clear financial structure. Tags: Use lightweight labels for grouping. Spaces: Use the location tree (Property → Room → Cabinet) to track exactly where items are.

4. Efficiency

Use global search to find items by name, brand, or tag. Multi-select items to enter batch edit mode, where you can quickly move items between spaces or update their status (e.g., "Gifted", "Depreciated").

5. Data Security

Your data stays local by default. With iCloud Sync enabled, data only moves within your private cloud. Regularly use "ZIP Export" to backup your entire library, including all attachments, to a safe location.
