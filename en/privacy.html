Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

1. Data Collection

MyVault is a "local-first" application. We do not run any backend servers, and we do not collect, store, or upload any of your personal asset data, photos, or text records through the app.

2. Data Storage & Sync

All your data is stored locally on your iOS device by default. If you enable iCloud Sync, your data will flow within your private iCloud space via encrypted channels provided by Apple. The developer has no access to this data.

3. Permissions

Photo Library: Used only when you actively choose to add photos of your items. Face ID / Touch ID: Used only for local app unlocking. Biometric data is protected by the system. iCloud Access: Used to sync your asset data across your own devices.

4. Third-Party Services

This app contains no third-party advertisements, trackers, or analytics plugins. We do not sell your data to any third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact the developer through the GitHub repository or the feedback channels within the app.
